So you want to be an Olympian?

Being an Olympian is one of the coolest and hardest accomplishments out there. In this project, we explore some of the factors you can possibly change to maximize your chances of being an Olympian.

Which Country should you represent?

USA leads in Olympic Medals

From 1896 - 2016, USA has won the highest number of Olympic Medals, far higher than any other country. You are more likely to win an Olympic medal from the US than any other country.

USA sends highest no. of Olympians

If you just want to be an Olympian, being an American is still your best bet since it sends the highest no. of athletes every time. Click on any country to get more details.

More Olympians means more Medals

This visualization helps you explore how different national factors correlate with Olympic medals won and total Olympians. The size of the bubble represents the factor you choose.
